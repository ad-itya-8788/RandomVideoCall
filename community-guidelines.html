Community Guidelines

Welcome to our Random Video Chat App! To ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone, we ask that all users follow these guidelines:

1. Be Respectful

Treat others with kindness and respect. Avoid using offensive, rude, or inappropriate language. Respect different opinions and perspectives.

2. No Harassment or Bullying

Do not threaten, intimidate, or engage in any form of harassment or bullying.

3. No Hate Speech or Discrimination

We do not allow content that promotes hate or discrimination of any kind.

4. No Explicit or Inappropriate Content

Do not share or engage in inappropriate or explicit content.

5. Respect Privacy

Do not share personal or sensitive information during chats.

6. No Spam or Advertising

Don't promote unrelated content, services, or send spam messages.

7. Report Violations

Report inappropriate behavior so we can take quick action.

8. Follow the Law

Engage responsibly. Illegal activities are not tolerated.

Failure to follow these guidelines may result in account suspension or permanent ban.
